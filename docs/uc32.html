Use Case: Scan Product (UC32)

Use Case ID: UC32

Use Case Name: Scan Product

Author: QuyNV

Version: 1.0

Date: 31/1/2026

Actor: Cashier

Aligned with code: casher/barcodeScanner/BarcodeScanner.html

Description

Allows the Cashier to scan or enter product barcodes (or search by name/code) to add items to the sales cart. The system looks up the product by barcode, adds it to the cart with quantity 1 (or increments existing line), updates the cart table and grand total. The cashier can also search products by name or barcode and select from the list to add to cart.

Precondition

  • PRE-01: The Cashier is logged in and has opened a shift (Open Shift completed).
  • PRE-02: The Cashier is on the Barcode Scanner (POS) screen.

Trigger

TRG-01: The cashier scans a product barcode with a scanner (keyboard wedge) or enters barcode in the input field, or searches for a product by name/code and selects it.

Post-Condition

  • POS-01: The product is added to the cart (new line with qty 1 or existing line qty incremented).
  • POS-02: The cart table and Grand Total are updated.
  • POS-03: The barcode input is cleared (when adding by barcode).

Main flows

StepActorAction
1CashierAccesses the Barcode Scanner screen (main POS).
2SMSDisplays the form: Customer Name, Phone (optional), barcode input ("Scan or enter barcode..."), "Scan product barcode" button, "+ Add To Cart" button, "Search product" input, "View search page" link; cart table (Product Name, Stock Qty, Unit Price, Qty, Subtotal, Remove); Grand Total; discount and payment section.
3CashierScans barcode (keyboard wedge fills barcode input) or types barcode in the barcode input field.
4CashierPresses Enter or clicks "+ Add To Cart" (or clicks "Scan product barcode" to focus the barcode field then scans).
5SMSLooks up product by barcode in the product catalog. If found: adds to cart (qty 1 or increments existing line), clears barcode input, re-renders cart and Grand Total. If not found: no change (barcode input remains).
6SMSCart table shows: Product Name, Stock Qty, Unit Price, Qty (with − / + controls), Subtotal, Remove button. Grand Total is updated.

Alternative flows

IDAt stepConditionActorAction
AT1Step 3–4Search by name/code instead of barcodeCashierTypes in "Search product" field; system shows matching products in a dropdown; cashier selects one; product is added to cart (qty 1 or incremented) and search input is cleared.
AT2Step 5Barcode not foundSMSProduct not in catalog; no item added. Barcode input is cleared on submit; user can enter a different barcode.
AT3Step 4Barcode input empty when "+ Add To Cart" clickedSMSFocus is set to the barcode input; no cart change.

Business Rules

IDRule
BR-01Adding a product by barcode adds one unit; if the product is already in the cart, its quantity is incremented by 1.
BR-02Cart lines show Product Name, Stock Qty, Unit Price, editable Qty (with − / +), Subtotal, and Remove. Grand Total is the sum of subtotals (before discount).
BR-03Barcode input is cleared after a successful add (Enter or Add To Cart). Search product list is cleared after selection.
